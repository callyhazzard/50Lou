Who we are

A Louisville gift shop that sells locally made art and artisan goods: jewelry, apparel, books, Kentucky-made food products, housewares, decor, art and quirky items all made in Kentucky.

Our Values

We want to do everything we can to uplift our community, and to show the world what a wonderful, weird place Louisville is.

Services we offer

The shop helps you put together custom gift "buckets" and they offer gift wrapping. 5-0-Lou has plenty of quirky products that make for great souvenirs. Bring the kids in for a free balloon and to create at our coloring table. 5-0-Lou is dog-friendly and keeps a clean water dish and snacks for furry friends. 5-0-Lou has an on-site print shop where they produce t-shirts with original designs by local artists as well as other printed goods. Custom t-shirts can be ordered and printed while you wait. 5-0-Lou is located on the Frankfort Avenue FAT Friday Trolley Hop route. The trolley drops shoppers off at our front door the last Friday of every month.
